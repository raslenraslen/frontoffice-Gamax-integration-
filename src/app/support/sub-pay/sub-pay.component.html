The automatic renewal of a subscription

When your subscription expires, it gets automatically renewed unless you cancel it beforehand. The next payment is charged without prior notice. As per our Terms of Use, we provide no refund upon the automatic renewal of a subscription. Unless you want to prolong your subscription, please cancel your current subscription in My Account at least 2 days prior to its expiration date.
